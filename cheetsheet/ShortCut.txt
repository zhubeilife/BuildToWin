Terminator
    1-Ctrl+Shift+ O/E
        Split terminals Horizontally/Vertically.
    2-Ctrl+Shift+ Right/Left/Up/Down
        Change the size of the window
    3-Ctrl+Shift+ F
        Search within terminal scrollback
    4-Ctrl+Shift+ N/P
        Move to the next or previous window.
    5-Ctrl+Shift+ C/V
        Copy and paste selected text to clipboard
    6-Ctrl+Shift+ W
        Close the current terminal.
    7-Ctrl+Shift+ Q
        Quits Terminator
